Welcome to Elysium Aerial Arts, Fitness and Wellbeing.

Based in the heart of Industrial Huddersfield and surrounded by the Colne Valley, with 3 large studios and a great variety of classes to help keep your mind and body healthy.

From a vigorous full body workout class to the more laid back Aerial stretch classes, there truly is something for everyone.

With our holistic approach in all our classes and a studio ethos of nurturing positive mental health as well as the physical, we believe that this will be an environment you will thrive in.

Aerial Hammock

With the hammocks low to the ground this is an excellent form of exercise for the complete beginner.

Being in the air takes away some of the pressure that normal stretching or floor yoga based moves can cause.

The hammock can aid in movement, strength and flexibility and allows people of all ages and abilities to feel like they have done an all over body workout.

Pole Dancing

Pole Dancing is becoming vastly more popular around the world as a form of exercise, as a hobby and as a great confidence booster. We have a variety of Pole classes ranging from Pole Fitness to Stripper Pole.

helps build upper body strength and improve the core.

Pole is a great cardio workout which is not only fun but addictive.

Aerial Hoop

Aerial Hoop is generally taught using a large metal hoop suspended in the air, moves are created holding, sitting and hanging on the equipment in creative dynamic sequences.

Aerial Hoop often called a Lyra truly is a full body workout and is great for your core, coordination and flexibility.

varying styles of Aerial Hoop make it accessible to everyone and promises to be a great confidence booster.

Easily one of the more prettier Aerial Arts, there will be lots of chances for you to snap a photo or two.

Aerial Silks

Silks uses long pieces of fabric suspended from the ceiling, in this class we teach you how to climb, invert and build strength, many moves are centred around the fabric and mostly in the air.

Silks will help develop confidence, build upper body strength, flexibility all while toning and increasing core strength.
